Cardboard Lunchbox

How can we design a lunchbox with limited supplies and no adhesives?

Timespan

4 Week Project, March 2017

Task

Design a cardboard lunchbox with limited materials and no adhesives.

Toolbox
  • Observation
  • Cardboard folding

    • Context

    The introductory design course required for all design majors was difficult but fun. I am not an industrial designer, but this was a project that introduced me to implementing research and problem solving techniques to fill an interesting requirement: using only a 15x18 sheet of eflute cardboard to design a lunchbox container.
